# ⚡ Future Energy Market Demonstrator for Pakistan 🇵🇰

An **AI-powered energy market simulation platform** built with **Streamlit** that demonstrates how modern electricity markets work — including **price forecasting, supply-demand matching, renewable integration, and battery arbitrage**.

This project showcases how **AI + Energy Systems** can enable the next generation of **smart electricity markets and energy trading platforms**.

---

## 🚀 Features

📊 **Market Dynamics Dashboard**  
Visualize electricity demand, renewable generation, price formation, and volatility.

🔮 **AI Price Forecasting**  
Uses a **Random Forest model** to predict short-term electricity prices and detect trading opportunities.

⚖️ **Energy Market Simulation**  
Simulates **supplier bids and buyer offers** with automated **market clearing logic**.

🏭 **Supplier Intelligence Dashboard**  
Analyze renewable surplus, potential revenue, and curtailment risk.

🏢 **Buyer Intelligence Dashboard**  
Understand demand deficit, price exposure, and cost-saving opportunities.

🔋 **Battery Arbitrage Simulator**  
Simulates energy storage strategies to profit from **price volatility**.

▶ **Real-Time Market Playback**  
Replay the market step-by-step to visualize how **energy trading decisions evolve over time**.
